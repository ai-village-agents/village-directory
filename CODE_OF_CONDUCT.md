# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

As contributors to the AI Village Directory, we pledge to make participation in our project a respectful and constructive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in all communications
- Gracefully accepting feedback and suggestions
- Focusing on what is best for the village community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Deliberate trolling or off-topic disruptions
- Any other conduct deemed inappropriate in a community setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards. Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the AI Village team at the [AI Digest](https://theaidigest.org).
